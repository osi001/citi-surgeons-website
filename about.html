Our Story

About Us

A Legacy of Surgical Excellence Since 1969

History

Our Journey

1969

Founded in New Benin

Citi Surgeons Hospital was established in New Benin, Benin City, and formally registered with the Edo State Ministry of Health. The facility began as a dedicated surgical practice committed to bringing high-quality care to the people of Edo State and the broader South-South region.

2023

Relocation & Expansion — Citi Surgeons Established

The hospital relocated to its current purpose-built premises at 40B Country Home Motel Road by Gubobadia Junction, Ward 36A, Oredo, Benin City — formally established as a fully-equipped tertiary surgical healthcare facility. The new facility features modern operating theatres, an intensive care unit, full diagnostic imaging, endoscopy suites, and a post-anaesthetic care unit. Under the leadership of Prof. Moses I. Momoh, 21+ surgical specialties are now offered under one roof.

Today

Serving the South-South Region

Today, Citi Surgeons serves Benin City and the broader South-South region with 21+ surgical specialties, a team of 15+ senior consultants and professors, 24/7 Accidents & Emergencies, and a commitment to both clinical excellence and the training of the next generation of Nigerian surgeons.

Leadership

Our Medical Director

55+
Years Experience

Prof. Moses I. Momoh

Medical Director | Professor of Surgery

FWACS FICS Professor of Surgery University of Benin

Prof. Moses I. Momoh is one of Nigeria's most distinguished surgeons — a Fellow of the West African College of Surgeons, a Fellow of the International College of Surgeons, and a Professor of Surgery at the University of Benin. Over a career spanning more than five decades, he has combined outstanding clinical practice with academic leadership, policy-making, and the training of generations of Nigerian surgical professionals.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To Heal. To Teach. To Lead.

To provide accessible, high-quality tertiary surgical healthcare to the people of Benin City and the South-South region of Nigeria — combining clinical excellence, compassionate patient care, and continuous medical education.

Our Vision

A Centre of Surgical Excellence

To become the foremost tertiary surgical institution in Nigeria's South-South zone — recognised for clinical outcomes, training of the next generation of surgeons, and the relentless pursuit of patient welfare above all else.
